INTERACTIVE MEDIA DESIGN


Learn about our program and how it prepares students for the industry.

"Unlock Your Digital Creativity Future with Us! Explore a world of digital design, development, and innovation in our two-year Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College . Why Choose Us? Gain a comprehensive skill-set in web design, coding, animation, and more. Access cutting-edge facilities and industry-standard software. Open doors to exciting career opportunities in various fields. Stay ahead with emerging technologies and industry-relevant tools!"

Read More

"Here's why our program stands out:

1. Comprehensive Skill-Set: Over two years, you'll dive deep into computer-based web design, programming, 3-D animation, and design for print. You'll also master in-demand skills like InDesign, coding, marketing, and media management.

2. State-of-the-Art Facilities: Located at Fanshawe’s London Downtown Campus, our program provides access to cutting-edge equipment and industry-standard software such as Adobe and Maxon. We also embrace open-source technologies for web application development, ensuring you're ready for anything the digital world throws your way.

3. Career Opportunities: As a graduate of our program, you'll be well-prepared to pursue a variety of exciting career paths. Opportunities abound in communication design studios, computer software development firms, interactive software publishers, media production companies, and IT consulting firms.

4. Emphasis on Emerging Technologies: Our curriculum is designed to keep you ahead of the curve. You'll learn about new media technologies, with a special focus on web application design, interactive rich media, and the latest tools for developing user-friendly, media-rich websites and applications.

5. Industry-Relevant Tools: We integrate industry-standard tools and software from renowned companies like Adobe and Maxon, ensuring that you're proficient in the tools used by professionals in the field"

CLIENT PROJECT WITH LEVEL 4 STUDENTS

Working in small groups and functioning as a mini-studio, level 4 students will design, develop, and pitch a professional marketing and advertising campaign.

The mission of Foundation Sixty 6 is to facilitate improved access to mental healthcare for youth between the ages of 16 and 25 suffering from psychosis, bipolar disorder (BPD), and schizophrenia, specifically within Southwestern Ontario.
