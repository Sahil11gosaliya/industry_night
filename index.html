EVENT DETAILS

The SDPA Industry Night for 2023 will showcase student projects and activities from the Interactive Media Design Program.

We extend a cordial invitation to an evening of professional engagement, featuring the presentation of Capstone Projects for Foundation Sixty 6 and Student Portfolios. Attendees will have the opportunity to peruse and discuss the showcased work while enjoying a selection of catered Food and Refreshments.

Dear, Industry Professionals,
This event serves as a valuable platform for networking with potential hires and industry peers. Take advantage of the occasion to engage in meaningful conversations, foster connections, and explore collaborative opportunities. Your presence at this event is instrumental in contributing to a mutually beneficial exchange of ideas and insights. We look forward to your participation in this evening dedicated to the celebration of academic accomplishments in the Interactive Media Design Program.
